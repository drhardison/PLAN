Events and Tournaments

The calendar for visual learners is at the bottom of this page. Scroll down to find it.
Times marked with Assorted Gaming indicate times during which the building/rooms will be open to the public.
Attendees are at liberty to entertain themselves during running tournaments once they have been eliminated or if they choose not to partake.
Attendees, however, are reminded that they may not be outside of the area we have set aside for PLAN.
Other rooms are allocated for alternative activities that may run during tournaments.
Regardless, here are the events and their info (in order of occurrence) for PLAN:


IMPORTANT: Tournaments will start when scheduled. Please arrive early to make sure you're signed in!


The Leauge of Legends Tournament and the CS:GO Tournament will take place simultaneously. Sorry:(

League of Legends Tournament

WHEN: Saturday, October 11, 2014 from 12pm EST until 12am EST.
DETAILS: The League of Legends Tournament teams can be reserved before-hand on this site or will be decided on an ad-hoc basis.
This tournament will be 5 vs. 5 - Summoner's Rift in a tournament-style draft.
The format of the tournament is double elimination with each round being determined by best out of 3 games.
Tournament members will take screenshots of wins and losses for validity.

Prizes for League of Legend Tournament:
1st Place: 3200 Riot Points and a Triumphant Ryze skin for each player.
2nd Place: 2400 Riot Points for each player.
3rd Place: 1600 Riot Points for each player.
4th Place: 800 Riot Points for each player.

CS:GO Tournament

WHEN: Saturday, October 11, 2014 from 3pm EST until 7pm EST.
DETAILS: The CS:GO Tournament teams can be reserved before-hand on this site or will be decided on an ad-hoc basis.

Team Fortress 2 Tournament

WHEN: Sunday, October 12, 2014 fromt 10am EST until 3pm EST.
DETAILS: The Team Fortress 2 Tournament teams can be reserved before-hand on this site or will be decided on an ad-hoc basis.
The tournament will be in the Highlander Format. If the teams are not of size 9, then each team may only have 1 of each class.

Super Smash Bros. Tournament

WHEN: Sunday, October 12, 2014 from 4pm EST until 9pm EST.
DETAILS: Our Super Smash Bros. tournament is brought to you with the help of KZOO Smash.
The tournament will be organized in a 1 vs. 1 bracket.
